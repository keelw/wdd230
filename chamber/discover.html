Herriman is a beatufiul city located on the southwest bench of the Salt Lake Valley. The city extends from the southern border of South Jordan to the far end of Salt Lake County. It is a beatufiul city that backs right up to the mountains.

On the east, the entire Salt Lake valley can be seen, including the stunning mountains that Utah is famous for.

20 years ago, Herriman was far disconnected from Salt Lake and was much more rural than it is now. The expansion of the I-15 freeway, Bangerter HWY, and Mtn View Corridors have made Herriman connected to the rest of the valley to it's north and Utah valley to it's South.

Although Herriman is a buzzing location for growing businesses and retail centers, there is still something rustic about it that also makes it a destination away from the large city centers of Salt Lake, Draper, Sandy, and West Jordan. There are fishing lakes and hiking trails riddled around the city.

Herriman's population, median income, property values, and household value have skyrocketed in the last several years.

The retail sector of Herriman, and nearby Riverton, have also been growing rapidly to match the needs of the increasing population.

This growth has been taking place on both sides of Herriman. The east side toward Riverton, as well as the northwest side towards South Jordan.
