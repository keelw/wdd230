Site Name:

City of Herriman Chamber - herrimanchamber.org

This site name was selected because I need something that is short and directly correlates with the purpose of the site. It needs to be concise and professional. This is also the typical/expected naming convention of many chamber of commerce sites.

Site Purpose:

The City of Herriman Chamber of Commerce website aims to:

  • Promote local businesses
  • Provide networking opportunities
  • Act as an information hub
  • Encourage community engagement
  • Advertise and promote

Scenarios:

  • How can joining the chamber of commerce benefit me as a small business?
  • Where can I find information about events that the chamber holds that I can attend?
  • How can I contact the chamber board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?
  • How can I participate?
